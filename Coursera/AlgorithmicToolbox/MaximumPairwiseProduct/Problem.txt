Given a sequence of non-negative numbers
find the maximum pairwise product

Input. The first line of the input contains number 2<=n<=2*10^5.
The next line contains n non-negative integers

Output. A single number - the maximum pairwise product

Sample 1.
Input
3
1 2 3

Output
6

Sample 2.

Input
10
7 5 14 2 8 8 10 1 2 3

Output
140
